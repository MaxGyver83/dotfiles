GIMP
====

m      move (sel/layer/path)
r      rectangle select
o      color picker
x      swap colors
B      bucket fill
g      gradient fill
C-S-n  new layer
PgUp   layer up
PgDn   layer down
C-a    select all
C-S-a  deselect all
Tab    hide docks
C-j    center image
C-S-l  cut and float
hold C-A and drag to move selected area
